Level Up Your Work

Finished your projects? Here are ideas to enhance your documents and push yourself further.

🎨 Colour Schemes

Pick 2–3 colours that work well together and use them consistently across your headings, borders and highlights. This makes your document look professionally designed.

📐 Alignment & Spacing

Use paragraph spacing (before and after) instead of pressing Enter multiple times. Set consistent line spacing (1.15 or 1.5) throughout your document for a polished look.

🖼️ Image Effects

Try picture styles — add shadows, reflections or rounded corners to your images. Use the crop tool to remove unwanted parts and make photos fit perfectly.

📊 Table Design

Use the Table Design tab to add alternating row colours (banded rows). Merge cells for a title row across the top. Remove or style individual borders for a custom look.

🔤 Font Pairing

Use one font for headings and a different font for body text — this is what professional designers do. Try pairing a bold sans-serif heading with a clean serif body font.

📑 Styles & Themes

Use Word's built-in Styles (Heading 1, Heading 2, Normal) instead of manually formatting each heading. This keeps everything consistent and lets you create a table of contents automatically.

💾 Save as PDF

When your document is finished, save it as a PDF (File → Save As → PDF). This preserves your formatting exactly and is how professionals share final documents.

🏆 Combine Skills

Create a brand new document of your own choice — a recipe book, a game guide, a music magazine — and use every skill you've learned across all the lessons.
